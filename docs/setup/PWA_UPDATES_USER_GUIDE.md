# How to Update Sommething PWA on iPhone

## Automatic Updates (New!)

As of version 3, Sommething now **automatically detects and notifies you** when updates are available!

### What You'll See

When a new version is ready:

1. A **red notification banner** slides up from the bottom of the screen
2. Message says: _"A new version of Sommething is ready"_
3. Two buttons appear:
   - **Reload Now** - Get the update immediately
   - **Later** - Dismiss and update when convenient

### How to Update

1. **Tap "Reload Now"** when the notification appears
2. App refreshes automatically
3. You're now on the latest version!

That's it! Super simple.

### Update Check Frequency

- Checks every 60 seconds when app is open
- Checks when you open the app
- Checks when you switch between pages

You'll typically see the update notification within 1 minute of a new version being released.

## Manual Update Methods

If you don't see the notification, or want to force an update:

### Method 1: Force Close the App

1. Swipe up from the bottom of your iPhone (or double-click Home button on older models)
2. You'll see all open apps
3. Find Sommething and swipe it **up** to close it completely
4. Go back to your home screen and tap Sommething to reopen
5. The app will load the latest version

### Method 2: Delete and Re-add

If force closing doesn't work:

1. **Long press** the Sommething icon on your home screen
2. Tap **Remove App** → **Delete App**
3. Open **Safari** and go to your Sommething URL
4. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow)
5. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
6. Tap **Add** in the top right

You'll have the latest version, and all your data is safe (stored in the cloud, not the app).

### Method 3: Clear Safari Data

If you want to completely reset:

1. Open **Settings** app
2. Scroll down to **Safari**
3. Tap **Clear History and Website Data**
4. Tap **Clear** to confirm
5. Open Sommething from your home screen

## How to Tell What Version You Have

Currently, the version isn't displayed in the app. But you can tell by checking for new features:

- **Version 1**: Basic wine tracking
- **Version 2**: Space switcher in header
- **Version 3**: Update notifications (this feature!)

## Troubleshooting

### "Update notification won't go away"

- Tap **Reload Now** to update
- If you dismissed it and it keeps coming back, you need to update
- The notification will persist until you update or a newer version is released

### "I updated but still see old version"

1. Try force closing (Method 1 above)
2. Clear Safari data (Method 3 above)
3. Delete and re-add the app (Method 2 above)

### "No update notification appearing"

- Updates might not be available yet
- Check that you have internet connection
- Try force closing and reopening the app
- Wait a few minutes - checks happen every 60 seconds

## Tips

- **Update when convenient**: The "Later" button lets you dismiss the notification if you're in the middle of something
- **Update before important use**: If you're about to track a special bottle, update first to ensure you have the latest features
- **Check occasionally**: If you don't use the app daily, you might miss updates. Force close periodically to refresh

## Questions?

If you have issues with updates or the app isn't working properly after updating:

1. Try the manual update methods above
2. Check your internet connection
3. Make sure iOS is up to date
4. Try deleting and re-adding the app (your data is safe!)
